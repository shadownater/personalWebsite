About Me

I'm an artist programmer, programming artist!
I'm a recent graduate from the University of Alberta with a degree in Specialization in Computing Science. I enjoy creating things, so I initially thought I would be completing a bachelor of Arts at my local college. My first year wasn't very satisfying, so I switched into the sciences. I craved the problem-solving I used to do in my math classes, but I still wanted to be creative in my field. Computing science was the best option, and it's really allowed me to combine my interests in the best way possible.
I can code in Java, C, Python, Javascript, Html, and Css. I think my favourite is Java, but lately I've been using Python mostly, so it I like that one a lot too. SQL. Unity.
I made this website in Django, and I used Bootstrap for the first time for the layout.
As for art, I mostly work digitally in Krita. I love Illustrator and Photoshop is pretty great too. I used to work as a graphic designer for a few summers between my studies. I personally draw my own characters or characters from anime/games.
Thank you for visiting!

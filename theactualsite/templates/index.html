About Me

My name is Jillian Lovas! I'm an artist programmer, programming artist! I'm a recent graduate from the University of Alberta with a degree in Specialization in Computing Science. I enjoy creating things, so I initially thought I would be completing a bachelor of Arts at my local college. My first year wasn't very satisfying, so I switched into the sciences. I craved the problem-solving I used to do in my math classes, but I still wanted to be creative in my field. Computing science was the best option, and it's really allowed me to combine my interests in the best way possible. I made this website in Django, and I used Bootstrap for the first time for the layout.

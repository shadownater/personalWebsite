About Me

I'm an artist programmer, programming artist!

I'm a recent graduate from the University of Alberta with a degree in Specialization in Computing Science. I enjoy creating things, so I initially thought I would be completing a bachelor of Arts at my local college. My first year wasn't very satisfying, so I switched into the sciences. I craved the problem-solving I used to do in my math classes, but I still wanted to be creative in my field. Computing science was the best option, and it's really allowed me to combine my interests in the best way possible.

I can code in Java, C, Python, Javascript, Html, and Css. I think my favourite is Java, but lately I've been using Python mostly, so it I like that one a lot too. SQL is fine, and I prefer to use SQLite. I have experience making games with Gamemaker and a little bit of Unity experience as well. Mostly, I've functioned as the artist for teams making games, but I've been working on my own game projects on the side where I can do both roles. Often, I am the only artist in a room of programmers, so I will fill the role where I'm most needed.

I made this website in Django, and I used Bootstrap for the first time for the layout. Please see my coding tab for more information.

As for art, I mostly work digitally in Krita. I love Illustrator and Photoshop is pretty great too. I used to work as a graphic designer for a few summers between my studies. I personally draw my own characters or characters from anime/games. Besides digital/traditional artwork, I make costumes as a hobby.

Thank you for visiting!
